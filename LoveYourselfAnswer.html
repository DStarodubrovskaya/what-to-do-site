Music: Love Yourself: Answer

March 05, 2024

Love Yourself: Answer

Artist: BTS
Genre: Pop, Hip Hop, R&B

My Opinion:
"In my opinion this is one of their best albums.
A perfect ending to the Love Yourself trilogy."

Love Yourself: Answer is BTS's third Korean-language compilation album, released on August 24, 2018, by Big Hit Entertainment. It comprises twenty-five tracks, including seven new songs, with "Idol" as the lead single. The album debuted at number one in South Korea, Canada, Japan, and the United States, marking BTS's second chart-topping album in the US.
Love Yourself: Answer made history by spending one year on the Billboard 200 chart, becoming the first Korean album to do so. Additionally, it received gold certification in the United States and silver certification in the United Kingdom. The album concludes the Love Yourself series, presenting a storytelling composition known as "起承轉結 (Kishōtenketsu)". The promotional campaign included trailers, concept photos, and performances on various music shows. Critics praised the album for its narrative depth and meaningful themes, cementing BTS's place in the genre.
