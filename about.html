Intro

My name is Daria Starodubrovskaya, id 345173645, a bachelor's degree student of information studies at Bar Ilan University.
And this is my final paper in the website construction course (35-730-02).

The website in front of you contains my personal recommendations for books, music and movies. I always liked to share interesting things I found with others. That's why I built the site, where anyone who trusts my taste can find an idea of what to do in their free time.

The site is called "What To Do" which for me is a name that speaks for itself. I also built him a unique logo that includes the letter W of the main font. The letter in the form of a human shrugging his shoulders.

The site has pages that make up collections (read, see, hear) with a brief description and pages that make up a detailed description of the items. The main page contains a section with recent recommendations, where you can find items recently added to each collection. The site is also translated into English.
