Music: Gorgorod

March 01, 2024

Gorgorod

Artist: Oxxxymiron
Genre: Rap, Grime, Political Hip-Hop

My Opinion:
"These are more than songs, this is a whole novel in verse.
I know it by heart."

"Gorgorod" is the second studio album by Russian rap artist Oxxxymiron, released on November 13, 2015. The album consists of 11 compositions and is conceptual, presenting a dystopian narrative set in the fictional city of Gorgorod. The protagonist, Mark, undergoes a journey of development influenced by ancient and biblical mythologies.
The album was recorded within two weeks, with Oxxxymiron as the sole lyricist and Porchy serving as the music producer for most tracks. Following its release, "Gorgorod" topped the charts on Russian iTunes and Google Play, maintaining its popularity for over two years and securing the eighth position among the most popular albums in 2017.

"Gorgorod" received mixed reviews from critics but captured the attention of music portals, hip-hop fans, scholars, literary critics, and public figures. In 2017, The Flow, a Russian hip-hop portal, included the album in its list of the 20 most important Russian hip-hop albums of the 2010s.
