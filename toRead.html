Books

February 27, 2024

Flowers for Algernon

Author: Daniel Keyes
Genre: Science fiction

My Opinion:
"Before reading: A mentally retarded becomes a genius. and a mouse.
After: I'm in tears. I'm crying like I don't know who. I burst out at the commas. And then until the end. Definitely a must read."

February 26, 2024

Galápagos

Author: Kurt Vonnegut
Genre: Novel, Science fiction

My Opinion:
"People have played it out. The apocalypse happened. Of all humanity, only 10 unfortunates remained.
In this book you will learn how they got to the island, and what humanity has evolved into over a million years."

January 31, 2024

The Technicolor Time Machine

Author: Harry Harrison
Genre: Science fiction

My Opinion:
"The film studio is facing a crisis that threatens bankruptcy. They decide to travel back in time and make a film about Vikings in real locations. With real Vikings, of course.
A very interesting surreal. Be sure to read and re-read!"
